Forward Declarations
====================
-it is a important concept and important at some situations
-Forward declartions is a specification of procedures or a functions that is defined in the package body
-it is called before the body for that procedures/functions that is defined.
-Basically it looks just like a specs which are done in the body it just tells the compiler that there is valid calls within the body which is defined at some points
