Foundation Tools
----------------
1)SAS Certified Base Programmer for SAS 9
Designed for programmers, analysts, data managers, or anyone writing SAS programs to access and manage data to perform queries and analyses

	SAS Programming 1: Essentials + SAS Programming 2: Data Manipulation Techniques 
	
2)SAS Certified Advanced Programmer for SAS 9
The next level for SAS Base Programmer for SAS 9 certified professionals and the foundation for many job roles
	SAS Programming 3: Advanced Techniques and Efficiencies + SAS Macro Language 1: Essentials + SAS SQL1: Essentials

3)SAS Certified Clinical Trials Programmer Using SAS 9
Appropriate for programmers who use SAS software to analyze clinical trials data to produce reports for submission to regulatory authorities

	SAS Programming 1: Essentials +SAS Programming 2: Data Manipulation Techniques +SAS Macro Language 1: Essentials + 
	SAS Report Writing 1: Essentials +Statistics 1: Introduction to ANOVA, Regression, and Logistic Regression
